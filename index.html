Our Story

A Family Legacy
Built on Trust

Founded in 2012 in the textile capital of Solapur, Bura Textiles is a family-owned manufacturing house with a single-minded commitment — creating towels that deliver real comfort with lasting quality.

With over a decade of hands-on experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in terry towel manufacturing, supplying premium-grade products across India.
