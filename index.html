Our Heritage

A Family Legacy
Woven in Every Thread

Founded in 2010 by Nagnath Mohan Bura and Balaji Mohan Bura, BURA TEXTILE has grown from a passionate local enterprise into a trusted name in premium terry towel manufacturing, serving bulk buyers and export markets across India and beyond.

The next generation has arrived — Rohit Nagnath Bura brings modern vision and operational expertise to expand and elevate the family legacy. Together, the Bura family continues to uphold an unwavering commitment to quality, craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction.

Operating from our state-of-the-art facility in MIDC Solapur, we deploy advanced rapier loom technology with meticulous in-house quality control — ensuring every towel that leaves our factory meets the standards of international markets.

Our Process

Advanced Manufacturing

Powered by cutting-edge rapier loom technology and decades of textile expertise, every towel passes through a rigorous in-house production pipeline.

Rapier Loom
01

Premium Cotton Sourcing

We source the finest long-staple cotton yarn from trusted suppliers, ensuring exceptional softness, absorbency, and consistent fibre quality for every production run.

02

Rapier Loom Weaving

Our advanced rapier looms interlace warp and weft threads with millimetre precision, creating the characteristic terry loop pile that defines premium towel performance.

03

Finishing & Quality Control

Every batch undergoes rigorous washing, drying, and finishing processes. GSM verification, dimensional checks, and visual inspection ensure consistent export-grade output.

04

Packaging & Dispatch

Towels are neatly packed, labelled, and prepared for bulk dispatch. We accommodate custom packaging and branding requirements for wholesale and export clients.

Our Advantage

Why Choose BURA TEXTILE

We combine decades of family expertise with modern manufacturing capabilities to deliver a product that speaks for itself in every international market.

In-House Production
Complete control over the manufacturing process — from yarn to finished product — ensures unmatched consistency and the ability to meet precise bulk order specifications.
Rapier Loom Technology
Advanced rapier looms deliver superior weave density, even pile height, and exceptional durability — producing towels that retain their quality through hundreds of wash cycles.
Export-Ready Quality
Every towel is manufactured to meet international export standards. Our finishing processes deliver the texture, weight, and presentation required by global buyers.
Premium Cotton Yarn
We use only the highest grade cotton yarn, ensuring superior softness, excellent moisture absorption, and long-lasting fabric consistency across every SKU.
Strict Quality Control
Multi-stage quality inspections — GSM checking, dimensional accuracy, pile density, and colour consistency — guarantee zero-defect product delivery for every bulk order.
Family Business Trust
As a family-owned enterprise with 15+ years of heritage, we build lasting relationships with our clients. Your order receives personal attention from order to delivery.
