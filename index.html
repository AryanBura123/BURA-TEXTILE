Our Legacy

A Family Woven
Into Every Thread

Founded in 2010 by Nagnath Mohan Bura and Balaji Mohan Bura, BURA TEXTILE was built on a foundation of passion, precision, and premium craftsmanship. Rooted in the heart of Solapur's thriving textile belt, the company has grown from its humble beginnings to become a trusted name in terry towel manufacturing.

Today, Rohit Nagnath Bura has joined the legacy, bringing fresh vision and energy to strengthen the family's commitment to quality and growth. Together, three generations of dedication continue to drive BURA TEXTILE toward global excellence.

With advanced rapier loom technology, strict in-house quality control, and premium cotton yarn sourcing, every towel we produce carries the weight of our family's promise — durable, beautiful, and built for the world.
